What Is a Donor-Advised Fund?

Understanding Donor-Advised Funds

A donor-advised fund (DAF) is a charitable giving account that allows you to contribute assets, receive an immediate charitable tax receipt, and recommend grants to qualified charities over time.

Contributions may be invested and grow tax-free within the fund. You determine when and how grants are distributed, allowing you to plan your giving strategically while aligning it with your broader financial goals.

Donor-advised funds are widely used by individuals and families seeking a structured and tax-efficient approach to philanthropy.
